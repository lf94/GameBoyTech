Bivertless - DMG ROM Palette Inverter

This is a proof of concept that allows you to take a DMG ROM and invert the colors. Do not expect it to work for every ROM. Bivertless has solely been tested on Tetris and a piece of homebrew...Both methods are a work in progress.

I do not advocate using this as a bivert chip replacement.
